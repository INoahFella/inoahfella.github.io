Services

Game Audio:
Game audio is an all-encompassing term for responsive sound, atmospheric audio, and even music. All the important components of a memorable piece of media.

Music Editing:
I offer mixing and mastering of your already completed pieces of audio. Compression, equalization, limiting, whatever you require!

Foley Audio:
Foley audio is any miscellaneous sound effects for media, things like ambient audio, footsteps, etc.

Composing:
Establishing a mood for your media is a crucial component of whether it will resonate with your audience. I'm here to make sure the right tone is established.

Music Transcription:
Need sheet music for a tune but can't seem to find any around? I offer transcriptions of any piece of music for your learning needs.
