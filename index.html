Offered Services

How can I help you?

Game Audio:

Game audio is an all-encompassing term for responsive sound, atmospheric audio, and even music. All the important components to take your game immersion to the next level.

Music Editing:

I offer mixing and mastering of your already completed pieces of audio. Compression, equalization, limiting, whatever you require!

Foley Audio:

Foley audio is any miscellaneous sound effects for media, things like ambient audio, footsteps, etc.

Composing:

Establishing a mood for your media is a crucial component that decies whether it will resonate with your audience. I'm here to make sure the right tone is established.

Music Transcription:

Need sheet music for a tune but can't seem to find any around? I offer transcriptions of any piece of music for your learning needs.
